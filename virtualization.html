Virtualization with VMware ESXi & VCSA

Configured a complete virtualization environment using VMware ESXi 6.7 and vCenter Server Appliance (VCSA). The setup included installation of ESXi, staging and deployment of VCSA, configuration of iSCSI & NFS storage, vApps, virtual networks (Standard & Distributed Switch), OVA/OVF templates, High Availability, DRS, and vMotion for load balancing and failover. Successfully managed VM templates, backups, and created a distributed and scalable lab simulation environment.

Key Tasks:

  • Installation and configuration of VMware ESXi 6.7 hosts.
  • Deployment and management of vCenter Server Appliance (VCSA).
  • Configuration of storage solutions: iSCSI and NFS shared storage.
  • Setup of virtual networks including Standard and Distributed Switches.
  • Implementation of High Availability (HA), Distributed Resource Scheduler (DRS), and vMotion.
  • Creation and management of VM templates and backups.
  • Designed scalable lab environment for testing and demonstrations.
