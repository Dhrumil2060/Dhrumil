NIST-CSF Strategy Implementation

Developed and implemented a tailored NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) strategy to enhance security posture for small to medium-sized enterprises.

Key responsibilities and achievements:

  • Conducted risk assessments aligned with NIST CSF core functions: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover.
  • Developed cybersecurity policies and procedures to address identified risks and compliance requirements.
  • Implemented continuous monitoring processes and trained staff on incident response workflows.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams to integrate security into business operations.

Tools and methodologies used:

  • NIST CSF Framework
  • Risk Management Tools
  • Security Policy Development
  • Incident Response Procedures
