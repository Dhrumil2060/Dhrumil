NIST-CSF Strategy Implementation

This project implemented the NIST Cybersecurity Framework strategy to help small businesses improve security posture by adopting foundational and prioritized controls.

  • Developed business questionnaires, asset inventory, risk registers, and NDAs.
  • Applied Bayesian risk modeling and SWOT analysis to prioritize efforts.
  • Leveraged CIS-O methodology to simplify and integrate control guidance.
  • Modeled a hypothetical small construction company to demonstrate approach.
  • Key tools included Open-AudIT, Wazuh, AWS Inspector, and Microsoft Defender for Cloud.
  • Final deliverables included NDAs, governance templates, and tailored NIST profiles.
