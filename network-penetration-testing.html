Network Penetration Testing

Project Overview:

Led a targeted penetration testing exercise on a controlled network lab to proactively identify security weaknesses and potential attack vectors.

Key Achievements:

  • Conducted detailed vulnerability assessments using automated and manual techniques.
  • Mapped network topology and discovered active hosts, services, and open ports.
  • Exploited identified vulnerabilities to demonstrate risk impact and potential breaches.
  • Analyzed captured network traffic for suspicious patterns and signs of compromise.
  • Documented findings with actionable remediation recommendations.

Tools & Technologies: Nmap, Wireshark, Metasploit, Kali Linux.
